Deep Knowledge Tracing

Deep Knowledge Tracing (DKT) uses Long Short Term Memory (LSTM) model to the problem of predicting student responses to exercises based upon their past activity. This models the knowledge state of a student and predict whether a student has sufficient knowledge to answer problems not yet seen. The learned model can be used for intelligent curriculum design, based on the current knowledge state of a student, resources can be suggested to students based on their individual needs, and content which is predicted to be too easy or too hard can be skipped or delayed and can give necessary feedback.
